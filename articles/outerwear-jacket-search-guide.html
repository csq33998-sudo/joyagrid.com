Outerwear

How to search jackets and outerwear by silhouette

Outerwear changes the entire outfit, so it deserves more careful searching than a generic jacket query.

Bomber jackets

Search bomber jacket, cropped bomber, nylon bomber, or washed bomber. Check sleeve volume, hem ribbing, zipper quality, and shoulder shape.

Windbreakers

Windbreakers should be judged by panel lines, fabric shine, hood shape, and how the hem falls. Add color terms if results feel too broad.

Varsity jackets

Look closely at sleeve material, collar ribbing, snaps, patches, and overall length. The jacket should not look too stiff or too flat.

Puffers

For puffers, check fill distribution, collar height, sleeve width, and whether the body looks boxy or tapered.

Work jackets and denim jackets

Search work jacket, chore jacket, carpenter jacket, denim jacket, or washed denim jacket. Texture and pocket placement matter here.
