Budget

How to estimate shipping and total cost

A low item price can look attractive until shipping, packaging, and service costs are included. Estimate the full spend so the final number does not surprise you.

Think in total landed cost

Total cost includes item price, domestic shipping if any, service fees, packaging, international shipping, and possible taxes or duties.

Weight changes the decision

Tees and small accessories are usually easier to add. Shoes, denim, hoodies, jackets, and bags can add weight quickly.

Packaging choices matter

Keeping shoe boxes can protect presentation but increases volume. Removing boxes may reduce shipping size, but it can change how the item arrives.

Consolidate with a plan

Combining several items may reduce per-item shipping cost, but only if you actually want everything. Do not add filler items just to feel like you are optimizing shipping.

Search lighter items

Start with pieces that are easier to evaluate and ship.
