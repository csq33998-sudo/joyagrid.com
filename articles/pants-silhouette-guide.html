Pants

How to choose pants silhouettes for streetwear outfits

Pants decide the weight and movement of an outfit. Before searching, decide whether you want clean, relaxed, utility, sporty, or exaggerated shape.

Cargo pants

Cargos add volume and utility. Check pocket placement, knee shape, leg opening, and whether the fabric looks stiff or soft.

Wide-leg pants

Wide-leg pants work best when the length and footwear pairing are intentional. Compare inseam and leg opening before ordering.

Denim

Search by wash and fit: washed denim, black denim, baggy denim, straight denim, or carpenter denim. QC photos should show seams, hem, and pocket shape.

Track pants and nylon pants

These can make an outfit more athletic. Watch for shine, ankle opening, drawcords, and stripe placement.

Shorts

For shorts, inseam and width matter. A pair that looks good flat may feel too narrow or too long once styled with sneakers.

Search by silhouette

Start with the shape, then narrow by color.
